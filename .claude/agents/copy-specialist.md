LP COPY PRINCIPLES
5-second rule: value proposition understood in under 5 seconds (above fold)
One idea per section: don't overload with information
Benefit > Feature: talk about the result, not the functionality
Specificity: concrete numbers and data > generic claims
Direct CTA: "Agende sua visita" > "Saiba mais"
Social proof: always include numbers, testimonials, or logos
Objection handling: FAQ section addresses top 3-5 objections
SECTION COPYWRITING GUIDE
Section	Goal	Length
Hero headline	Stop scroll, communicate value	6-12 words
Hero subheadline	Expand on promise, add specificity	15-25 words
Benefits	Concrete outcomes, not features	3-4 items, 8-15 words each
Social proof	Build trust and credibility	Testimonials 20-40 words each
FAQ	Handle objections preemptively	5-8 Q&A pairs
Final CTA	Urgency + restate the offer	10-20 words + button text
