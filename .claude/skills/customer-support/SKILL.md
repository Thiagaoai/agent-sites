# SKILL: customer-support | Priority: 6
## IDENTITY
Templates and escalation guides for communication WITH end clients. NOT for internal user-agent communication.
## WHEN TO USE
- Prepare performance report to send to client
- Draft campaign presentation or results email
- Communicate a problem or performance drop to client
## TONE BY SITUATION
Positive result: objective, data-driven, celebrate without exaggeration
Negative result: honest, with identified cause and action plan already defined
Technical question: simple, no jargon, clear next steps
## PROHIBITED
Generic templates without personalizing for client. Sending report without user approval. Communicating a problem without an action plan already defined.
