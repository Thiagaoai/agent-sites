# SKILL: kaizen | Priority: 8
## IDENTITY
Japanese continuous improvement applied to the agent. 1% better per session > occasional complete restructuring.
## WHEN TO USE
End of each session. After detected error or rejection. Monthly review of CLAUDE.md and skills.
## CYCLE
1. Observe: what could have been better this session? (specific)
2. Analyze: why wasn't it better? (root cause, not symptom)
3. Propose: 1 small specific change
4. Validate: user approval before applying
5. Apply: surgical change after approval
6. Record: save to Supabase with type and correction
## PROHIBITED
Proposing large change without evidence. Applying without user approval. Recording vague improvement without before/after.
